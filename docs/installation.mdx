# Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install our music software on your system.

## System Requirements

- **Operating System**: Windows 10/11 or macOS 10.15+
- **Processor**: Intel i5 or equivalent (i7 recommended for complex projects)
- **RAM**: 8GB minimum (16GB or more recommended)
- **Storage**: 20GB free space (SSD recommended)

## Installation Steps

1. **Download the Installer**
   - Visit our official website and download the installer for your operating system.

2. **Run the Installer**
   - Double-click the downloaded file to start the installation process.

3. **Follow the Prompts**
   - The installer will guide you through the process. Choose your installation directory and select any additional components you want to install.

4. **Activate Your License**
   - Once installed, launch the software and enter your license key when prompted.

5. **Set Up Audio Preferences**
   - Go to Settings > Audio and configure your audio interface settings.

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during installation, please check our [FAQ section](/faq) or contact our support team.
